Sharnbasava University

A Private University enacted by Govt. of Karnataka as "Sharnbasva University Act 2012" Karnataka Act No.17 of 2013.
Notification No. ED 144 URC 2016 dated 29/07/2017.

Cources we offer

Intermediate

Our institution recognizes the importance of bridging the gap between foundational knowledge and advanced studies. That's why we are pleased to present our comprehensive intermediate courses, carefully crafted to provide students with a solid understanding of their chosen field.

Degree

Our degree programs are crafted to provide students with a comprehensive and enriching educational experience. We offer a diverse array of majors, ensuring that students can pursue their passions and tailor their education to their unique career goals.

Post Graduation

Our postgraduate education goes beyond traditional boundaries, providing a platform for advanced study and research in various disciplines. At Sharnbasava University, you'll have the opportunity to work closely with renowned faculty members who are experts in their fields.

What Our Student says

I've found my academic journey to be incredibly enriching and transformative. The university provides a stimulating and inclusive environment that encourages critical thinking and innovation. The faculty members are not only experts in their fields but also passionate about guiding students towards success. The diverse range of courses and extracurricular activities has allowed me to explore my interests and develop a well-rounded skill set. The university's commitment to research and hands-on learning has provided me with valuable practical experiences, preparing me for the challenges of the real world. Additionally, the supportive community at Sharnbasava University has made my time here truly memorable. From engaging lectures to collaborative projects, I feel well-equipped and confident as I approach graduation. I'm proud to be a part of this academic community that fosters growth, curiosity, and a commitment to excellence."

Raashi

The college provides an environment that encourages both academic excellence and personal growth. The faculty members are not only knowledgeable but also genuinely invested in the success of their students. The small class sizes allow for meaningful interactions, fostering a sense of community and collaboration. The range of extracurricular activities and student organizations has given me the opportunity to explore my interests beyond the classroom, making my college experience truly holistic. The career services offered by the college have been invaluable in preparing me for the professional world. Overall, Sharnbasava University has been the perfect blend of academic rigor, supportive community, and a platform for self-discovery. I'm grateful for the opportunities and friendships forged during my time here."

Aryan

About Us

Established with a vision to provide a transformative education, our college is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity, creativity, and a sense of responsibility. At the heart of our institution are our distinguished faculty members, who bring a wealth of expertise to the classroom and inspire students to think critically and push the boundaries of knowledge.
